Community Guidelines



Respect and Conduct

  • Show Respect to All Members

    Treat everyone with kindness and respect. Discrimination, harassment, or any form of abusive behavior will not be tolerated.

  • Act Ethically in All Interactions

    Uphold integrity and honesty in your actions and communications. Misleading or deceptive behavior is unacceptable.

  • Use Common Sense in Your Behavior

    Make thoughtful decisions and exercise good judgment. If something feels wrong, it probably is.

  • No Hacking or Unfair Advantages

    Do not use any hacks, cheats, or modifications that provide an unfair advantage over others. This includes but is not limited to, using hacked clients, scripts, or exploits.

Interaction and Communication

  • Avoid Causing Excessive Grief or Harm to Others

    While some level of griefing and stealing is allowed, do not take it to extremes. Refrain from actions that cause severe emotional or physical distress to other members. This includes excessive trolling, bullying, and creating major conflicts.

  • Respect Personal Belongings

    Although some theft is permitted, respect the hard work of others. Do not engage in excessive or destructive stealing that ruins the experience for others.

  • NSFW Content is Strictly Prohibited

    Any discussion, sharing, or promotion of not safe for work (NSFW) content, including explicit or offensive material, is not allowed under any circumstances.

  • No Hacking or Exploiting

    Do not engage in or promote hacking, cheating, or exploiting game mechanics to gain an unfair advantage.

Content and Contributions

  • No Spamming or Advertising Allowed

    Avoid posting repetitive, irrelevant, or unsolicited messages. Advertising your products, services, or other websites is not permitted without prior approval.

Enforcement and Moderation

Moderators and Admins Will Enforce Rules at Their Discretion

The moderation team reserves the right to enforce these guidelines as they see fit. Actions may include warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans, depending on the severity of the violation.

By following these guidelines, you help create a positive and enjoyable environment for everyone. Thank you for being a part of our community!
